Our trainings will ensure that teachers, students and parents have an enjoyable and pedagogically solid experience with Eduten Playground.

Training

Using Eduten Playground does not require any special IT-skills from teachers or students. It has been designed to be very easy to use and you can easily get started by watching our three minutes Getting started -video. However, our trainings do not just concentrate on which buttons to press but gives you a solid pedagogical approach how to leverage the full potential of digital pedagogics and learning analytics with Eduten Playground. The training process has been perfected over the years with thousands of teachers. It has a very practical approach giving you tools for your daily work right away. All our trainings can be given either online or in person.

Advanced Training

The first step is the Basic training. It's a 90 minute workshop that gives you the bare essentials to understand the pedagogical approach of Eduten Playground and get started with your students right away. The basic training is followed by weekly usage of Eduten Playground. This is the best way to get familiar with the platform and it creates a solid foundation to get the most out of the advaned training. The training culminates in the advanced training. During the 90 minutes workshop you'll learn more about learning analytics, different ways of differentiating the students, customising the content and most importantly how to motivate your students.
